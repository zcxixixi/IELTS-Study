# IELTS Writing Parts Library
# 零件库：可复用句式与表达

A collection of high-value sentence templates for IELTS Writing Task 2.

---

## 立场表达（Thesis / Position Statements）

| 句式 | 用途 | 备注 |
|------|------|------|
| `its benefits far outweigh its drawbacks` | 表明偏向正面的立场，预告观点方向 | 考官友好；立场清晰；适用于 discuss both views + opinion 题型 |

**示例：**
> While some people have concerns about social media, I believe **its benefits far outweigh its drawbacks**.
